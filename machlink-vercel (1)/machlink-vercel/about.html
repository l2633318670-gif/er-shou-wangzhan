About MachLink China

We are a China-based sourcing agent for used milling machines. We connect buyers in Africa, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East with quality second hand machines from Chinese factories.

Who We Are

MachLink China is a sourcing agent based in China. We specialize in finding, inspecting, and exporting used milling machines from China to buyers around the world — especially in Africa, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East.

We are not a manufacturer. We are a bridge between you and the Chinese market. China has thousands of factories, workshops, and machine dealers with quality second hand milling machines at very low prices. The problem is most international buyers don't know how to find them, inspect them, or ship them safely.

That is what we do. We go to the factories. We inspect the machines. We send you videos and honest reports. Then we handle all the shipping and export documents. You get a tested machine at a fair price — without the risk.

Our focus is simple: honest communication, fair price, and working machines. We only offer machines we would be proud to stand behind.

How We Work

Our process is simple and transparent. No middlemen. No hidden fees.

1

You tell us what you need

You contact us on WhatsApp. Tell us the type of milling machine you need, your budget, and your country. The more detail you give, the better we can help.

2

We search our network

We have connections with dozens of used machine dealers and factories in China. We search for the right machine that matches your requirements and budget.

3

We inspect and film the machine

We visit the machine personally or work with our trusted partners. We check the condition, run the machine, and record a full video. You receive the video on WhatsApp.

4

You confirm and pay

After you see the video and confirm you want to buy, you pay. We accept T/T bank transfer. We give you a clear invoice before any payment.

5

We ship to your port

We pack the machine properly, prepare all export documents, and arrange ocean freight shipping to your port. We send you the tracking and all documents needed for import clearance.

Why Buyers Trust MachLink China

Buying used machinery internationally is risky. Here is how we reduce that risk for you.

🎥

Video Before Payment

We never ask you to pay for a machine you haven't seen running. You always get a video first. This protects your money.

📋

Honest Condition Reports

We tell you the truth about the machine. If there is a small problem, we say so. We explain what can be fixed and what cannot. No hidden surprises.

📞

Direct Communication

You talk directly to us — the people who inspect the machines. No call centers. No sales teams. Fast WhatsApp replies with real information.

📄

Full Export Documents

We provide all the documents you need: commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, and certificate of origin. Your import should be smooth.

🌍

We Know Your Market

We have shipped to many countries in Africa, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East. We understand local import requirements and can give you useful shipping advice.

💬

After-Sale Support

After your machine arrives, you can still contact us. If you have questions about operation, spare parts, or technical issues, we try to help.
