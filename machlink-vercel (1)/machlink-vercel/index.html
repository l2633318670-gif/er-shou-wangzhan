Are machines tested before shipping?+
Yes. Every used milling machine is tested before it leaves China. We check the motor, spindle speed, table movement (X, Y, Z axes), and overall mechanical condition.

Yes, video inspection is free. We record the machine running. You see the spindle, the table moving, and the full exterior condition — sent to you on WhatsApp before any payment.
